About this data

charley.vtk: CTA scan (Computed Tomography Angiography) of a healthy male
subject. The subject has had a contrast fluid injected into the blood stream
that increased the contrast of the "lumen". Data origin: Charles Taylor,
Biophysics department, Stanford University.

CT_HAND.vtk: CT scan of a human hand.

InnerEar.vtk: CT scan of a human inner ear.

sinus.vtk: CT scan of a human head, focussed on the sinus cavities.

marnix_small.mhd/.raw: CT scan from the Osirix collection of data.
